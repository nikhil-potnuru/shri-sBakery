Our Story

Bringing authentic Indian flavors to your neighborhood since 2020

The Beginning

Shri's Bakery was born from a passion for authentic Indian baking and a dream to bring the finest traditional recipes to Chelmsford. What started as a small family venture has grown into a beloved local establishment, serving our community with love and dedication.

Our journey began with time-honored family recipes, carefully preserved and perfected over generations. Today, we combine these traditional methods with modern baking techniques to create unique, delicious treats that bridge cultures and bring people together.

Our Commitment

At Shri's Bakery, we're committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and food safety. Every item is crafted with care, using the finest ingredients and time-tested techniques. We believe in creating not just delicious treats, but memorable experiences for our customers.
